Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging code means trying to reconcile overlapping changes to commits on different branches, whereas rebasing means moving or rewriting the commit history of a branch. Merging preserves the entire history of the repository, but rebsaing creates a linear history, moving the branch straight onto main.
